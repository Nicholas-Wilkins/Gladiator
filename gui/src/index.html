Welcome to Gladiator

Gladiator is a self-improving chess engine that trains champions through evolutionary competition. New challengers are generated and face the reigning champion — winners take the crown and the cycle continues.

Quick Reference

Dashboard — Overview of all engine variants and their champions. Shows active training sessions.
Training — Start training sessions. CPU Heuristic is fastest; Neural Network learns patterns but needs a GPU. Multiple workers train in parallel.
Champions — View champion stats, lineage, and match history. Export a bot to play against, or promote to MUTATION mode.
Play — Play chess against exported bots. Low=2, Medium=3, High=4 depth search. Click or drag to move pieces.
RANDOM mode — Challengers are generated from scratch each generation.
MUTATION mode — After 100 random wins, challengers become mutations of the champion. Training focuses on refinement.
